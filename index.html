“Horizon Commodities Group” LLC was established on February 28, 2020. Nevertheless its new established company in Azerbaijan, specialized colleagues of our company have great experience in European and CIS states and closely cooperate with large-scaled companies such as Gazpromneft, Tatneft, Belneftxim and Rosneft, engaged in petroleum products and lubricants.

Employees of our company are high-specialized staff with multi-branched education in the sphere of trade of petrochemical and petroleum products. Direct contracts on delivery and developed logistics enable to offer the customer quality products with sustainable and competitive prices. The company's logistics is so flexible that it allows the customer to deliver goods in both bulk and specialized packaging without increasing the price of the products offered. Key commercial names of the company are petroleum and lubricants of leading manufactures both in Russia and Europe. In general, our company wishes to present quality product to the largest consumers through flexible payment and discount system.
